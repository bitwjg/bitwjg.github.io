About Me

I am a Principal Engineer and Director of the Large Language Model team at Meituan. Before that, I was a senior algorithm engineer at Alibaba DAMO Academy. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in computer science from the joint Ph.D. program between Beijing Institute of Technology and Purdue University in 2016. My research interests include natural language processing, information retrieval, and machine translation.

My team is currently working on recursive self-improvement and automated research for large language models. We are looking for motivated interns and full-time researchers to join us. Please feel free to reach out if you are interested.
